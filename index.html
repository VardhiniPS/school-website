About My School

At Crayons - The Colourful Beginning, we believe every child is like a unique crayon, capable of creating a beautiful masterpiece. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where children between 2.5 to 6 years can grow, learn, and explore their fullest potential.

We offer a PRE-K Programme that focuses on holistic development, ensuring each child receives exposure to rich vocabulary, engaging storytelling, and interactive conversations. With our affordable half-day and full-day sessions, we create a space where your little ones feel comfortable, secure, and excited to learn.

Why Choose Crayons?

At Crayons, we understand that 90% of brain development occurs in the first five years of life. That's why we prioritize creating an enriching atmosphere to lay a strong foundation for their future.

Our team of experienced and caring teachers helps enhance fine motor skills while encouraging creativity and exploration. We also emphasize outdoor activities, giving children exposure to sunlight for better health and resilience. With a balanced combination of indoor and outdoor play, theme-driven classrooms, and stimulating puzzles and toys, we ensure your child's journey is both fun and educational.
